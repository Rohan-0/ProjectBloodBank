About

We, at RedFLOW aim to connect hospitals with their nearby blood banks so that hospitals would never run out of their blood reserves. Moreover, registered hospitals can quantitatively and qualitatively analyse the stored data (of blood donors) of nearby blood banks without any breach of blood-donor's personal information and also predict future donations with the help of same.
